# CRM Website UI/UX Design Project

## Project Overview

This project presents the UI/UX design of a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) website. The platform is designed to help businesses efficiently manage leads, clients, tasks, team members, and business analytics through a centralized dashboard. The design focuses on usability, productivity, and a clean professional interface.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to create a user-friendly CRM system that streamlines customer management, sales tracking, team collaboration, and business performance monitoring.

## Key Features

* Secure User Authentication
* Lead Management
* Sales Pipeline Tracking
* Client Management
* Task Assignment and Tracking
* Team Collaboration
* Business Analytics and Reporting
* User Settings and Customization

## Screens Designed

### 1. Login Screen

* Secure user authentication
* Email and password login interface
* Clean and professional welcome screen

### 2. Dashboard Home

* Overview of key business metrics
* Recent activities and notifications
* Quick access to important modules

### 3. Leads Management

* View and organize potential customers
* Add, edit, and track lead information
* Search and filter leads efficiently

### 4. Lead Pipeline

* Visual representation of sales stages
* Track lead progress from prospect to conversion
* Drag-and-drop workflow management

### 5. Client List

* Comprehensive list of clients
* Search, filter, and sort functionality
* Quick access to client records

### 6. Client Profile

* Detailed client information
* Contact details and interaction history
* Associated tasks and notes

### 7. Task Manager

* Create and assign tasks
* Monitor task progress and deadlines
* Improve team productivity and organization

### 8. Analytics

* Business performance insights
* Sales and lead conversion metrics
* Interactive charts and reports

### 9. Team Management

* Manage team members and roles
* Monitor team activities and responsibilities
* Facilitate collaboration and communication

### 10. Settings

* User profile settings
* Notification preferences
* System customization options

## Design Process

### Research

Conducted research on modern CRM platforms to understand user requirements and industry best practices.

### Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes to establish layout structure and navigation flow.

### UI Design

Developed high-fidelity designs using a clean layout, consistent components, and professional color schemes.

### Prototyping

Designed interactive user flows demonstrating lead management, client tracking, and team collaboration processes.
